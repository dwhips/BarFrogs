Bar Frogs

Rules

  1. Draw or steal a card at the start of your turn.
  2. Play the cards in your hand during your turn.
    • There isn't a limit to the number of cards you can play. You do not have to play any cards during your turn.
  3. Once your turn is over the next player's turn begins.
  4. The game ends once the deck is empty.
  5. The player with the 'Lucky Frog' card at the end of the game wins.
  6. The player with the 'Blackout' card at the end of the game loses.
